Move like a ninja

Ninjas are the masters of stealth, and for that crouching is Key. Always crouch while moving for maximum stealth. Who needs speed when you can sneak up on your opponents like a well-trained ninja? Bonus points if you crouch-walk the entire match!

But ninja can also be fast! You can also run straight to the ennemies and shoot your bullets at them whille running like if you were throwing shuriken at them. Channel your inner Usain Bolt and sprint straight into the fray, bullets blazing and adrenaline pumping. Who needs subtlety when you can unleash a barrage of bullets while hurtling through the air like a ninja missile? Sure, it might not be the most strategic approach, but hey, who needs strategy when you've got raw speed and sheer determination on your side?
